About Our Work

Each morning, our artisans begin with inspiration drawn from nature, traditions, and the joy of creating for others. Ideas are shaped into beautiful products using time-tested methods.

Once the products are ready, we pack them with multiple layers of love and care. Our packaging team ensures that each order reaches you safely and beautifully presented.

Over 250+ products are crafted, packed, and shipped daily across India. Our logistics team guarantees quality delivery to every corner of the country, every single day.

At KindleCraft we love and look for perfectly imperfect objects that combine simplicity of function and aesthetics.

2019    2020    2021    2022    2023    2024

2019: KindleCraft began as a humble dream shared by local artisans. We focused on traditional handmade decor and soft launches at local fairs.

2020: Despite challenges, we moved online and created our first e-commerce platform. Orders began flowing from all corners of India.

2021: The year of expansion. We added accessories, personalized gifting options, and reached 5,000+ customers nationwide.

2022: Onboarded 50+ new artisans, launched a sustainable packaging initiative, and expanded to international shipping.

2023: KindleCraft was featured in 3 design magazines, introduced its mobile app, and shipped over 50,000 products successfully.

2024: We continue to innovate with AI-assisted design previews, product customization, and celebrate a family of 100+ team members.
