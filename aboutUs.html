What is KindleCraft?

KindleCraft is a creative marketplace and a passion project born to promote handmade artistry and sustainable creations. We offer a wide range of handcrafted items including home décor, fashion accessories, paintings, woolen products, and thoughtful gifts curated from talented artisans across India.

Working since 2019, KindleCraft has grown into a trusted platform known for originality, authenticity, and environmental responsibility. Each product tells a story, and every purchase supports local artisans and empowers them to keep their traditions alive.

Our Journey

KindleCraft began in a small studio in Jaipur, Rajasthan, in 2019, with a team of just five passionate artists and creators. In our first year, we handcrafted and sold over 350 unique products across India—ranging from vibrant home decor pieces to traditional woolen items.

As word spread and appreciation grew, we expanded our collections, onboarded skilled artisans from rural areas, and grew to a team of 12 by 2021. By the end of 2022, we had successfully sold over 15,000 handmade items and introduced eco-conscious packaging and pan-India shipping.

Today, KindleCraft proudly operates with a team of 25+ members including product designers, wool artists, packaging leads, online managers, and customer care specialists. We have shipped over 60,000 handcrafted items to satisfied customers not only in India but internationally. Our goal remains rooted in preserving Indian traditions while crafting a sustainable future.

Meet the Founder

Natasha Aggarwal

Founder & Creative Director

With a deep love for creativity, sustainability, and Indian craftsmanship, Natasha founded KindleCraft in 2019 with a vision to empower artisans and bring handcrafted goods into the modern world. Coming from a design background and years of experience in the creative arts industry, she combined her aesthetic sensibility with business acumen to create a platform that not only sells products—but celebrates tradition and culture.
